Spiderman Social Feed





Project Objectives:

  • Recreate the social feed feature from Spider-Man PS4 in Unreal Engine
  • Create the social feed in a way where designers can simply alter spreadsheets to add extra social posts

Project status: Complete



Tools:

  • Unreal Engine 4 (Blueprint)
  • Photoshop

Creating a post is currently tied to pressing buttons, however, the social feed is setup using interfaces so the user can easily create new social posts by sending interface messages at any point they choose and inputting the necessary information. This will then take a random post and any replies that have been created, from the chosen category and add them into the social feed.


When recreating the social feed, I approached it from the perspective of a collaborative project. Due to this, I wanted to create it in a manner that would allow other designers to add new social posts by simply adding them into an external spreadsheet.
